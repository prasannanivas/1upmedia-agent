Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2025

  • Website interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you via Google Sign-In

3. Limited Use & No Sale

We access Google user data only to provide the features above. We never sell, rent, or share this data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Protection

  • OAuth 2.0 flows secured with PKCE
  • All traffic encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • Data at rest encrypted with AES-256
  • Quarterly security audits & least-privilege staff access

5. User Choices

  • Revoke access at any time in your Google account Permissions.
  • E-mail privacy@1upmedia.com to delete or export your data.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. Upon deletion, all Google-sourced data is erased from backups within 30 days.
